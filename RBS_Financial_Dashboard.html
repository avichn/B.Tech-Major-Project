Financial Overview

Per-vehicle investment and return metrics — adjust sliders above to see real-time impact

Key Finding: The Petrol Basic ISG retrofit delivers a payback of 3.3 years, 29% IRR, and a 10-year NPV of ₹8,133 with zero subsidy. This directly addresses the financial feasibility critique. The social abatement cost is negative — society gains ₹16,266 per tonne CO₂ averted, compared to FAME-II's ₹40,000–80,000/tonne.
